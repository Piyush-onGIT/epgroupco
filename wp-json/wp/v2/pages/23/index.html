HISTORY<\/h1><\/h2>\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t
\n\t\t\t\t
\n\t\t\t\t\t\t\t

In 2009, Ethix Pharma was founded by the pharmacologist Mr. Yogendra Kumar Choudhary emerging from his previous medical affairs job. Clinical trials have been conducted in multi-speciality study sites since the inception.<\/p>\n

At first, scientific consulting with regards to pharmacology, toxicology and kinetics was the main focus, specifically the design, execute and report writing as per GLP for herbal medicinal products. Further the characterization and documentation of herbal medicinal substances to produce monographs, white papers and regulatory dossiers were delivered.<\/p>\n

Later, consulting expanded towards Health Supplements, Nutraceuticals, Food for Special Dietary Use, Food for Special Medical Purpose, Functional Food and Novel Food. In particular, our vast expertise in the areas of Health Claims makes EP one of the leading consultancies.
