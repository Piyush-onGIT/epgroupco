MEDICAL WRITING & PUBLICATIONS <\/h3><\/h2>\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t
\n\t\t\t\t
\n\t\t\t\t\t\t\t

Changing market dynamics, expiring patents, and depleted product pipelines are all exerting pressure on healthcare companies to outsource non-core activities in an effort to control costs and enhance drug-development cycle time. one of these activities is medical writing\u2014an area that, although not at the core of operations, has become increasingly critical as regulatory agencies have adopted more elaborate review measures that require substantial amounts of documentation.<\/p>\n

Medical writing involves creating well-structured scientific documents that include clinical research documents, CTD, content for healthcare websites, health magazines, journals and news. Medical writing at Ethix Pharma has been classified into two types: regulatory medical writing and educational medical writing.\n

Regulatory medical writing involves writing documents for regulatory agencies seeking approval for devices, drugs and biologics. This includes non-clinical, clinical trial data, regulatory submission documents, post approval documents, etc.<\/p>\n

Educational medical writing is writing documents about devices, biologics and medicine, specifically for general studies.<\/p>\t\t\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t<\/section>\n\t\t\t\t
