The ultimate substantiation<\/h1><\/h2>\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t
\n\t\t\t\t
\n\t\t\t\t\t\t\t

Ethix Pharma is a full-service Clinical Research Organization (CRO) with a focus on natural health products. We have conducted more than 75+ clinical trials with moderately active substances as characteristic in most natural products.<\/p>\n

We provide support in the development of scientifically rational study designs covering a broad range of clinical trial types and indications. Alignment of scientific validity, regulatory requirements, and business needs is the basis for every study performed. We perform clinical trials from small-scale consumer acceptance studies to multinational, multi-site double-blind RCTs. Investigational products can be food products, medical devices and medicinal products. Services include planning of clinical studies to substantiate intended claims, identifying the right study population and assessments, successful recruitment due to our own study site including a subject database and network of investigators, and the generation of study documents and integrated reports and publications.<\/p>\t\t\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t<\/section>\n\t\t\t\t

CLINICAL TRIALS<\/h3><\/h2>\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t
\n\t\t\t\t
\n\t\t\t\t\t\t\t

At Ethix Pharma, our strength lies in our expertise in developing scientifically rational study concepts for natural products. Compared to synthetic drugs, natural products are often characterized by small effect sizes and by a delayed onset of the effects. The selection of the study population, the assessment parameters (including documentation), the course of study, and the analytical methods have to address these particularities.\nTo reflect different client needs, study purposes, regulatory frameworks and budgets, Ethix Pharma has implemented a flexible quality management system (QMS). Each study is assigned to one of three categories which define the level of quality management that is applied.\nWe work in close collaboration with largest multispecialty hospital, medical colleges\/universities and Public research organizations (PRO\u2019s) in India. Thus, we can offer a state of art medical facility, diagnostic and bed unit capacities.<\/p>\t\t\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t<\/section>\n\t\t\t\t
