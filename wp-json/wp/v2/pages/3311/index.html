\n\t\t\t\t\t\t\t

At Ethix Pharma a career means joining the agile consulting team with dedicated project work while staying abreast of the global regulatory situation to offer clients robust advice while dealing with national authorities within the various natural health categories.<\/p>\n

We work with team of scientist and experts from various fields to bring their best in the product development and research to this dynamically changing market. Attention to details, curiosity and commitment for our project success shape our outcomes for clients. Joining us at clinical projects means you will be a part of clinical investigation, committed to the substantiation of claims, to proving safety and efficacy, or to conducting post-marketing studies.<\/p>\n

In any case, it means joining a team of like-minded individuals who care about natural products and the success of our clients.<\/p>\t\t\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t
