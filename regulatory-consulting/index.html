Regulatory Consulting

Regulatory Consulting

Market meets science meets regulations With a unique combination of technical, clinical and regulatory expertise in natural health products, we provide a broad range of services in the areas of

  • Health Supplements
  • Nutraceuticals
  • Food for Special Dietary Use
  • Food for Special Medical Purpose
  • Functional Food and Novel Food.
  • OTC, ASU drug products and traditional herbal medicinal products
  • Cosmetics
  • Phyto-pharmaceuticals

HEALTH SUPPLEMENTS, NUTRACEUTICALS AND FOOD

Our consulting team offers competent and flexible services on all regulated food products, food ingredients and food improvement agents.

NOVEL FOOD, ADDITIVES, ENZYMES AND FLAVOURINGS

Our services include regulatory evaluations and applicable gap analysis, scientific support and consulting on conducting all types of required studies to the generation and submission of technical dossiers.

CLAIMS ON FOOD AND FOOD SUPPLEMENTS

We offer our expertise in science, regulations, and clinical research to support scientific marketing information, claim strategies for health and nutrition claims or beauty-from-within claims. We also advise on the use of other optional claims such as clean labelling, sustainability or traceability.

FOOD FOR SPECIFIC GROUPS (FSG),

E.G. FOOD FOR SPECIAL MEDICAL PURPOSES (FSMP)

Our services include the regulatory evaluation and strategic advice, scientific support and execution of required studies, as well as product notifications and the generation of technical dossiers.

OTC, ASU DRUG PRODUCTS AND TRADITIONAL HERBAL MEDICINAL PRODUCTS

Photo-ingredients (Extracts) are used in many foods including supplements and in herbal medicinal products. For extracts in herbal medicinal products, the most common way to market entry is a simplified registration, either as Traditional Herbal Medicinal Product (THMP) or as Well-Established Use (WEU) product.

HERBAL DRUGS

We provide the full range of services required for the registration of herbal medicinal products and the development of new drug products. Our services include consultations on all aspects of phyto-chemistry, pharmacology, toxicology, pre-formulation, formulation, new drug delivery and clinical research followed by regulatory support.

SELF-MEDICATION PRODUCTS

Our regulatory and medical affair services cover the entire range from OTC drug development to product registrations.

COSMETICS

The highly innovative cosmetics and personal care market is characterized by continuous formulation changes. Novel ingredients and changing regulations require constantly assessments of compliance.

COSMETIC INGREDIENTS AND FINISHED PRODUCTS

We assist in full service research of cosmetic ingredients and provide services related to the notification of finished cosmetic and personal care products.

Ethix Pharma is one of the agile consultancy and clinical research providers within the healthcare domain having valued experience in natural health products.
